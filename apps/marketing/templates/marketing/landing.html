Frequently asked questions

What if my patients don't use WhatsApp? +

92% of Indian smartphone users are on WhatsApp — that's higher than SMS, email, or any app. If a patient uses WhatsApp to send photos to their family, they can book an appointment with you. Zero learning curve.

Do I need to train my staff? +

No. Your receptionist doesn't interact with the bot at all — it runs on its own. They just see confirmed appointments in your existing schedule. 5-minute walkthrough included in setup.

How do I cancel? Am I locked in? +

Zero lock-in. No contract. Cancel anytime with a WhatsApp message to us — we turn off your bot and your data is deleted within 30 days. The 30-day free pilot is truly free — no card required upfront.

Is patient data safe? +

Yes. We store only name, phone, and appointment details — no medical history or diagnoses. Data is encrypted in transit (HTTPS) and at rest. Built on Meta's WhatsApp Business Cloud API with the same security clinic apps use.

What if the bot crashes or gives wrong info? +

We monitor it 24×7. If anything breaks, I personally get a WhatsApp alert and fix it — usually within the hour. During the free pilot you have my direct number. Patients can also message you directly from the bot if something's unclear.

Can it work with my existing clinic software? +

The bot runs standalone with its own admin. If you already use Practo / Lybrate / a spreadsheet, we can export bookings daily to Google Sheets (Standard + Premium plans). Deeper integrations on request.
