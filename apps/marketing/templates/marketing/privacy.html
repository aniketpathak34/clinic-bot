DocPing ("we", "us", "our") is a WhatsApp-based appointment booking service for clinics in India, operated by Aniket Pathak from Pune, Maharashtra. This Privacy Policy explains what personal data we collect, why we collect it, how we use it, and your rights under India's Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 (DPDP Act).

By messaging our WhatsApp bot or visiting any clinic page hosted by us, you consent to the practices described below. If you do not agree, please do not use the service.

1. Who is the data fiduciary

Aniket Pathak, operating as DocPing, is the data fiduciary under the DPDP Act for personal data collected through this service.

2. What data we collect

We only collect the minimum information needed to book and manage clinic appointments:

CategoryExamplesSource
Identity Name, WhatsApp phone number, preferred language You, when you message the bot
Appointment Doctor selected, date, time slot, clinic visited You, during the booking flow
Communication Messages sent to/from the bot, reminders received You + WhatsApp Business API

We do not collect: medical history, diagnosis, prescriptions, payment details, biometric data, or location data.

3. Why we use your data (purpose of processing)

  • To book, reschedule, or cancel clinic appointments you request
  • To send you reminders about your upcoming appointment (one day and one hour before)
  • To notify the clinic provider about your booking
  • To respond to enquiries you send to the bot
  • To improve the bot's accuracy in understanding your preferred language

We do not sell your data, do not use it for advertising, and do not share it with insurers, pharmaceutical companies, or marketing networks.

4. Lawful basis (DPDP Act)

  • Consent — when you message the bot, you give consent to process your data for the purpose of booking your appointment.
  • Legitimate use (Sec 7 DPDP Act) — sending appointment reminders is a legitimate use directly tied to the service you requested.

6. Where your data is stored

Personal data is stored on servers operated by Render, with database replication in their managed PostgreSQL service. Render's data centers are located in the United States and Europe. We rely on standard contractual clauses for cross-border transfer as permitted under the DPDP Act and the Indian Information Technology Act.

8. Your rights under the DPDP Act

You have the right to:

  • Access a copy of all personal data we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate or outdated data
  • Erase ("be forgotten") your data — see our data deletion page
  • Withdraw consent at any time (you can stop messaging the bot or block its number on WhatsApp)
  • Grievance redressal — file a complaint with the Data Protection Board of India if we don't respond within 30 days

To exercise any of these rights, email aniketpathak34@gmail.com with the subject line "DPDP Request — <your name>". We will respond within 30 days as required by law.

9. Children

Our service is not directed at children under 18. If a parent or guardian books an appointment for a minor, only the booking details (date, doctor, time) are stored — no health data. If you believe we hold data about a minor without parental consent, contact us and we will delete it immediately.

10. Security

We use TLS 1.2+ for all connections, encrypt data at rest in our database, scope database access to authorised personnel only, and use rotating API keys for third-party integrations. No system is perfectly secure; in the event of a breach we will notify affected users and the Data Protection Board within 72 hours.

11. Cookies and tracking

Our public website uses a session cookie for the admin login only. The marketing landing pages and the WhatsApp bot do not use any tracking cookies, analytics, or third-party advertising pixels.

12. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "last updated" date at the top reflects the most recent revision. Material changes will be communicated to active users via WhatsApp.
